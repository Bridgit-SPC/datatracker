### Prompt for AI Image Generation (Midjourney/DALL-E/Stable Diffusion)

```
A modern, professional illustration showing the concept of internet governance and meta-layer infrastructure. The image should convey:

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Abstract network nodes connected in a layered architecture
- Bottom layer: traditional internet infrastructure (geometric, technical)
- Top layer: interface/metadata layer (more organic, human-centered, glowing connections)
- A document or draft paper (ML-Draft-001) floating in the center, with light emanating from it
- Diverse, abstract human silhouettes around the edges, representing community participation
- Clean, minimalist design with ample white space
- Gradient from deep blue/purple (bottom) to warm orange/gold (top) suggesting evolution/progress

MOOD & TONE:
- Optimistic and forward-looking
- Professional yet approachable
- Collaborative and inclusive
- Technical but not intimidating
- Clean, modern, trustworthy

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: Deep blue (#1a237e) to purple (#4a148c)
- Accent: Warm gold/orange (#ff6f00) to yellow (#ffc107)
- Background: Clean white or very light gray
- Text overlay area: Semi-transparent dark overlay for readability

TEXT OVERLAY AREA:
- Leave space in center or bottom third for text
- "MLTF Datatracker - Early Access" or "ML-Draft-001"
- "Help Build the Next Internet" or "Building the Next Level of the Internet"

STYLE REFERENCES:
- Modern, clean technical standards aesthetic (professional)
- Modern SaaS product illustrations
- Abstract tech diagrams
- Community/collaboration imagery
- No photorealistic elements, keep it illustration-based
```

## Design Specifications

### Typography (for text overlays)

**Primary Headline:**
- Font: Bold, sans-serif (e.g., Inter Bold, Montserrat Bold)
- Size: 48-72px
- Color: White or dark blue (#1a237e)
- Weight: 700-900

**Subheadline:**
- Font: Medium sans-serif
- Size: 24-36px
- Color: White or medium gray
- Weight: 500-600

**Body Text:**
- Font: Regular sans-serif
- Size: 16-20px
- Color: White or dark gray
- Weight: 400

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Consistency:** Use same color palette across all images
2. **Text Overlay:** Always ensure text is readable (high contrast)
3. **Branding:** Include "MLTF" or "rfc.themetalayer.org" subtly
4. **Versions:** Create multiple versions for A/B testing
5. **Accessibility:** Ensure color contrast meets WCAG AA standards
